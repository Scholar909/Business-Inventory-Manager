Help & User Guide

Welcome to the App

This app helps you manage creditors, paid creditors, products, and jottings. Below is a detailed guide on how to use each section of the app.

1. Creditors

How to Add a Creditor:

  • Go to the creditors section.
  • Click on "Add Creditor" and enter the required details.
  • Click "Save" to store the creditor's information.

How to Search for a Creditor:

  • Use the search bar to find creditors by name only.

How to Edit a Creditor:

  • Click the edit button by the creditor's details in the list.
  • Modify the details as needed and click "Save".

How to Delete a Creditor:

  • Click the delete button next to the creditor's details.
  • Confirm deletion. This action is permanent.

How to Mark a Creditor as Paid:

  • Click the "Mark as Paid" button next to the creditor's name.
  • The creditor will move to the Paid Creditors section.

2. Paid Creditors

How to View Paid Creditors:

  • Go to the "Paid Creditors" section to see the list.

How to Search for a Paid Creditor:

  • Use the search bar to find paid creditors by name.

How to Delete a Paid Creditor:

  • Click the delete button next to a paid creditor's name.
  • Confirm deletion. This action is permanent.

3. Products

How to Add a Product:

  • Go to the products section.
  • Click "Add Product" and enter the necessary details.
  • Click "Save" to store the product information.

How to Search for a Product:

  • Use the search bar to find products by name or category.

How to Edit a Product:

  • Click on the product name in the list.
  • Modify the details as needed and click "Save".

How to Delete a Product:

  • Click the delete button next to the product name.
  • Confirm deletion. This action is permanent.

4. Jottings

How to Use the Jotting Section:

  • Use this section to take quick notes.
  • Notes are automatically saved.

How to Clear or Delete Jottings:

  • Click "Clear All" to remove all notes.
  • Click the delete button next to a specific note to remove it.
  • All deletions are **permanent** and cannot be undone.

Warnings

  • Deleting a creditor, paid creditor, product, or jotting is **permanent** and cannot be undone.
  • There is no restore section for deleted items.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Can I search for creditors by category? No, creditors can only be searched by name.
  • Can I search for products by category? Yes, you can search for products by both name and category.
  • Can I restore deleted items? No, deleted items cannot be restored.
  • Can I recover cleared jottings? No, once jottings are cleared, they are permanently removed.
